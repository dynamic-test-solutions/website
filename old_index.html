About Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS)

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS) was founded in 2003 by a team of individuals with vast experience in the semiconductor loadboard design business. Headquartered in Singapore, the company is structured to support the design, fabrication and assembly of both custom and generic PCBs for wafer and package test applications and provide complete turn-key ATE hardware solutions.

Global Service & Support

DTS has built its reputation on providing outstanding customer service. Taking that service and support directly to the customer in locations throughout the world is what differentiates DTS from the competition. DTS has built a global organization that includes sales, customer service, applications engineering, design centers, PCBs fabrication, assembly and Quality Assurance in all key geographic regions.
