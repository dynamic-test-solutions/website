  • DTS founded..

    DTS founded by a team of highly experienced semiconductor test design engineers

  • DTS Acquired..

    DTS acquired the Loadboard business unit of Kulicke & Soffa. (included the loadboard units of former companies; Probe Technology, Performance Test, Cerprobe, CompuRoute and OZTek). This acquisition enhanced both the DTS personnel as well as transferring many years of historical databases, tester templates, libraries, and other intellectual properties dedicated to the ATE board and design business. DTS continues to support old part numbers produced by the former companies obtained thru acquisitions.

  • Established..

    Established an Asian headquarters in Singapore under the name of Dynamic Test Solutions Asia Pte Ltd (DTSA). DTSA was established to provide full design and manufacturing support to all customers in the Asia Pacific region. Satellite offices have been established in the region to provide local support in various countries throughout Asia.

  • Established DTSA..

    Established DTSA Philippines office located in Alabang. The DTSA Philippines facility provides local sales support, customer service and design engineering to the Philippines customer base.

  • DTS Philippines..

    DTS Philippines established in Alabang

  • DTS acquired..

    DTS acquired by Tessolve Services (India)

  • Dynamic Test..

    Dynamic Test Solutions Asia acquires all shares from Tessolve and becomes an independent company. Corporate headquarters are relocated to Singapore, where engineering, manufacturing operations, logistics and corporate management reside.

  • DTS Re-establishes..

    DTS re-establishes its USA support team, including direct sales, applications engineering, design and customer service. On March 1st, DTS signs an exclusive agreement with Nikad Electronics Ltd for the sales, support and distribution of all DTS products in Europe and Israel.

  • On July 1st..

    On July 1st, 2013, DTS announces the merger with MC Test Products. The merger with MC Test will expedite the launch of DTS into the Silicon Valley Bay Area. MC Test is headquartered in San Jose California and was established in 1984. The combination of DTS and MC Test will greatly enhance their global presence and support network to the semiconductor test market.

  • DTS Completes..

    DTS completes acquisition of MCTest Products (San Jose, CA)
