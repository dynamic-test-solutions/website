DTS Core Products - Package Test Loadboards/DIB

DTS custom loadboards (DIB) are designed to specific devices and configured for both hand test and fully automated handler applications. DTS loadboards incorporate any brand of socket or contactor and can be configured for multi-site testing. Designs incorporate all necessary components, connectors, mechanical hardware and stiffeners to provide a complete plug-n-play solution. DTS has a vast database of tester and handler information which allow designs to be started and completed quickly without burdening the customer to supply excessive information. DTS designers are experienced in all device types, including digital, analog, mixed signal and RF devices.

Established a European headquarters in France under the name of Dynamic Test Solutions Europe S.A.S. (DTSE). DTSE was established to provide full design and manufacturing support to all customers in the European region. DTSE will locate designers in strategic locations throughout Europe to increase the level of service and support for its global operations. The addition of DTSE completes the key locations to fulfilling a global support network for DTS customers worldwide.

Established an Asian headquarters in Singapore under the name of Dynamic Test Solutions Asia Pte Ltd (DTSA). DTSA was established to provide full design and manufacturing support to all customers in the Asia Pacific region. Satellite offices have been established in the region to provide local support in various countries throughout Asia.

Acquired the Loadboard business unit of Kulicke & Soffa. (included former companies;Probe Technology, Performance Test,Cerprobe,CompuRoute and OZTek). This acquisition enhanced both the DTS personnel as well as transferring many years of historical databases, tester templates, libraries, and other intellectual properties dedicated to the ATE board and design business. DTS continues to support old part numbers produced by the former companies obtained thru acquisitions.

Global Service & Support

DTS has built its reputation on providing outstanding customer service. Taking that service and support directly to the customer in locations throughout the world is what differentiates DTS from the competition. DTS has built a global organization that includes sales, customer service, applications engineering, design centers, PCBs fabrication, assembly and Quality Assurance in all key geographic regions. Strategic headquarters have been established in the US, Asia and Europe with data systems linked and common processes and procedures to give the customers the benefit of working with direct DTS personnel in their specific locations, language and time zones. Multinational customers benefit by receiving the same support and service in all world areas. Access to databases and documentation from any DTS location is critical to supporting customer operations throughout the globe. DTS field operations include sales, customer service and applications engineers to work face-to-face with customers in all locations throughout the world. DTS also provides on-site, resident engineers to its major customers for continuous technical support.

DTS Quality

DTS takes Quality serious and employs a series of QA/QC checks throughout the entire process. Beginning at the initiation of a new job, we carefully check all customer supplied inputs, conduct multiple quality checks thru the design process and provide a comprehensive QC inspection of the final data package. DTS CAM Engineers utilize Tibor Darvis software with custom scripts to execute an automated and thorough QC of the CAM files, prior to releasing the project for fabrication.
