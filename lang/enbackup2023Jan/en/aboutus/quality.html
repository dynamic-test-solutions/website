Our Quality


Industry leading quality is a key DTS differentiator. We employ a series of QA/QC checks throughout the entire process. Our engineers utilize various software with custom scripts to execute an automated and thorough QC of the CAM files prior to releasing the project for fabrication. All DTS boards are fabricated in ISO9000 certified PCB fabrication facilities.
We have a 50+ point QA checklist that is completed on every job.

DTS stands behind its products and warrants all boards to be free of all defects for
a period of 90 days after shipment!
