News

2015


October Acquisition of New Singapore Office Completed

Dynamic Test Solutions Asia (DTS), a Singapore private limited company, has announced that they have completed the acquisition of a new office in Singapore. All staff and equipment moves were completed last month.

“We are pleased that we have been able to complete this acquisition and upgrade our corporate headquarters”, commented Todd Schnack, President & CEO of DTS. “All of our Singapore engineering, manufacturing, sales, logistics, finance, and management personnel are located in this new facility. The acquisition, versus leasing space, reflects another milestone in our long term commitment to Singapore.”


           

April New Taiwan Sales Partner Named

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS), a Singapore private limited company, has announced that they will further extend the offering of their industry leading products and services to the Taiwan market. To expand their presence in Taiwan DTS has partnered with Dyna Group International Electronics Co., Ltd. (Dyna Group). Dyna Group was formed in 2011 and provides semiconductor test related products and services to some of Taiwan's leading manufacturers. Initial delivery of DTS products to new Taiwan customers via Dyna Group is effective May 1st, 2015.

September ATE Hardware Supporting 28G Delivered

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS), a Singapore private limited company, has announced that they have developed a load board to support 28G test applications. Initial deliveries have started to a key U.S. customer.

“We are excited to deliver our initial 28G solution on schedule”, commented James Stanley, DTS Director, North America Business Unit. “This major design win will enable DTS to leverage our worldwide engineering and design resources across all of our customer locations.”

JulyAcquisition of MC Test Completed

"We are excited to deliver our initial 28 solution on schedule", commented James Stanley, DTS Director, North America Business Unit. "This major design win will enable DTS to leverage our worldwide engineering and design resources across all of our customer locations."

“We are pleased that we have been able to complete this transaction ahead of schedule”, commented Todd Schnack, President & CEO of DTS. “The products and services that we can now provide our increased global customer base moving forward are significantly broader. We are also now well positioned to accelerate our expansion into the key Silicon Valley market.”

JunePete Andreyev Named to Board of Directors      

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS), a Singapore private limited company, has named Mr. Pete Andreyev to the company’s Board of Directors. Mr. Andreyev has 35 years of leadership experience including at multinational corporations IBM, Hitachi, and General Electric. He has served on the boards of many public and private companies, including as chairman.

“We are excited to have an individual with Mr. Andreyev’s credentials join our board”, commented Todd Schnack, President & CEO of DTS. “He brings a new perspective to DTS and his broad experience will be valuable in assisting our company to achieve our aggressive business growth objectives.”

SeptemberTeseda Partnership Announced      

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS), a Singapore private limited company, has announced that they have partnered with Teseda Corporation. Teseda delivers a combination of bench-top test hardware with powerful software tools resulting in industry leading semiconductor device analysis solutions. DTS will become Teseda's exclusive provider of test hardware and will also participate in joint marketing and customer support activities effective immediately.

JulyMerger with MC Test Announced      

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS) a Singapore private limited company and MC Test Products, Inc., a California corporation, have announced that they will merge. The two companies are leaders in the design and fabrication of ATE and non-ATE semiconductor test hardware including final test loadboards, wafer test hardware, laboratory boards, and turn-key solutions.

“We are very excited about the synergies of our merger with MC Test”, commented Todd Schnack, President & CEO of DTS. “The products and services that we will be able to provide our combined global customer base moving forward become significantly broader. The merger will also accelerate our expansion into the key Silicon Valley market.”

Mr. Schnack, with 30 years of experience in semiconductor test hardware, will be the President and CEO of the merged Company.

SeptemberAll Outstanding Shares from Tessolve Services Acquired      

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS), a Singapore private limited company, has announced that they have acquired all outstanding shares from Tessolve Services and re-established DTS as an independent company and supplier to the global semiconductor ATE test market.
