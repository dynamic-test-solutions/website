Our Business

Dynamic Test Solutions (DTS) provides custom high performance tester interface hardware to a broad range of semiconductor automated test equipment (ATE) and laboratory board customers, including many in the Fortune 500.



DTS Business Model

  • Global Presence, providing a single partner worldwide and delivering Asia cost advantages
  • Comprehensive Engineering Knowledge of semiconductor applications and testing
  • Flexible Customer Solutions, including complete turn-key alternatives

DTS Product Offerings

  • Final test loadboards (DIB, DUT boards, HIB) & wafer test hardware (probe cards, PIB)
  • Lab boards (FA, bench, design verification, reliability)
  • Turn-key solutions (sockets, stiffeners, interfaces)
